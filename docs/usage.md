# Usage Guide

## Account Management

### Sign Up
1.  Navigate to the Sign Up page.
2.  Enter your details (Name, Email, Password).
3.  You will receive an OTP in your email.
4.  Enter the OTP to verify your account.

### Sign In
1.  Navigate to the Sign In page.
2.  Enter your email and password.
3.  Click "Sign In".

## Chat Features

### Creating a Chat
1.  Click on the "New Chat" button or icon.
2.  Search for a user by username.
3.  Select the user to start a 1-on-1 chat.
4.  For group chats, select multiple users and give the group a name.

### Sending Messages
1.  Open a chat from the sidebar.
2.  Type your message in the input field.
3.  Press Enter or click the Send button.
4.  **Images**: Click the attachment icon to upload and send an image.

### Managing Chats
-   **Delete Chat**: You can delete a chat from the chat options menu.
-   **Group Info**: View group members and leave the group if needed.

## Profile
-   Go to the Profile section to update your profile picture or change your password.
